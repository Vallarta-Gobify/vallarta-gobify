# Getting Started with Gobify

Welcome to Gobify, your comprehensive government administration platform. This guide will help you get started and become familiar with the system.

## What is Gobify?

Gobify Admin Portal is a modern web-based platform designed to help government agencies efficiently manage:

- **Citizen Information**: Comprehensive database of citizens with searchable profiles
- **Social Programs**: Federal and local social assistance programs with beneficiary tracking
- **Geographic Data**: Streets and neighborhoods for accurate address management
- **Reports**: Citizen-submitted reports and requests
- **Analytics**: Real-time statistics and trends through an intuitive dashboard

## System Requirements

To use Gobify, you need:

- A modern web browser (Chrome, Firefox, Safari, or Edge - latest versions recommended)
- Internet connection
- Login credentials provided by your system administrator
- Screen resolution of at least 1024x768 (1920x1080 recommended for optimal experience)

## Logging In

### First-Time Login

1. Open your web browser
2. Navigate to the Gobify portal URL (provided by your administrator)
3. You will see the login screen

   [Screenshot: Login page with email and password fields]

4. Enter your credentials:
   - **Email**: Your assigned government email address
   - **Password**: Your secure password (provided by administrator)

5. Click the "Sign In" button

### Password Security

- Keep your password confidential
- Never share your login credentials
- If you suspect your account has been compromised, contact your administrator immediately
- Change your password regularly (if prompted by the system)

### Forgot Password?

If you've forgotten your password:

1. Click the "Forgot Password?" link on the login page
2. Enter your registered email address
3. Check your email for password reset instructions
4. Follow the link to create a new password

## Understanding the Interface

After logging in, you'll see the main Gobify interface. Let's explore each component:

[Screenshot: Main dashboard with labeled interface elements]

### Top Navigation Bar

Located at the very top of the screen, the navigation bar contains:

1. **Gobify Logo**: Click to return to the dashboard from anywhere in the system
2. **Dark Mode Toggle**: Switch between light and dark themes for comfortable viewing
3. **User Profile Menu**: Access your profile settings and logout option

### Main Navigation Tabs

Below the top bar, you'll find the main navigation tabs:

- **Dashboard**: Home screen with key statistics and metrics
- **Ciudadanos**: Citizen management and search
- **Programas Sociales**: Social programs management
- **Calles**: Street directory and management
- **Colonias**: Neighborhood directory and management
- **Escanear QR**: QR code scanner for quick access
- **Reportes**: Citizen reports and requests

Click any tab to access that section of the system.

### Breadcrumb Navigation

[Screenshot: Breadcrumb trail showing "Dashboard > Programas Sociales > Program Details"]

When navigating deeper into the system, breadcrumbs appear below the main tabs showing your current location:

```
Dashboard > Programas Sociales > Programa Federal de Becas
```

Click any breadcrumb link to quickly navigate back to that level.

### Main Content Area

The center of the screen displays the current page content:
- Data tables
- Forms
- Maps
- Detailed information panels

### Search and Filter Tools

Most sections include search bars and filters at the top of the content area to help you find specific information quickly.

## Navigating the System

### Using Tabs

The simplest way to navigate is by clicking the main tabs:

1. Click **Ciudadanos** to manage citizen information
2. Click **Programas Sociales** to work with social programs
3. Click **Dashboard** to return to the home screen

### Using Breadcrumbs

When you're viewing detailed information:

1. Look at the breadcrumb trail at the top
2. Click any previous level to go back
3. Example: From "Program Details" click "Programas Sociales" to return to the program list

### Using the Back Button

Your browser's back button works throughout the system, but using tabs and breadcrumbs is recommended for a better experience.

## Dark Mode

Gobify offers both light and dark color themes for comfortable viewing in any environment.

### Switching Themes

1. Look for the theme toggle in the top-right corner of the screen
2. Click the toggle to switch between light and dark modes
3. Your preference is automatically saved

**When to Use Dark Mode:**
- Working in low-light environments
- Reducing eye strain during extended sessions
- Personal preference

**When to Use Light Mode:**
- Well-lit office environments
- Printing or screenshots
- Personal preference

## User Profile Menu

Access your profile settings and account options:

1. Click your profile icon in the top-right corner
2. A dropdown menu appears with options:
   - **Profile Settings**: Update your personal information
   - **Change Password**: Update your password
   - **Logout**: Sign out of the system

### Logging Out

To safely exit the system:

1. Click your profile icon (top-right corner)
2. Select "Logout" from the dropdown menu
3. You'll be returned to the login screen
4. Always logout when finished, especially on shared computers

## Understanding Your User Role

Your account has specific permissions based on your role:

### Federal-Only Users
- Can view and manage federal social programs only
- Cannot access local/municipal programs
- Full access to citizen data and geographic information

### Local-Only Users
- Can view and manage local/municipal social programs only
- Cannot access federal programs
- Full access to citizen data and geographic information

### Full Access Users
- Complete access to both federal and local programs
- All system features available
- Administrative capabilities (may vary)

**Note**: If you need different access levels, contact your system administrator.

## General Usage Tips

### Saving Your Work

- Forms typically auto-save or have clear "Save" buttons
- Look for confirmation messages after saving
- If you see unsaved changes warnings, don't navigate away until saving

### Loading Times

- Large datasets (10,000+ citizens) may take a moment to load
- You'll see loading indicators while data is being retrieved
- Be patient and avoid clicking multiple times

### Browser Recommendations

- Use a modern, updated browser for best performance
- Clear your browser cache if you experience unusual behavior
- Allow pop-ups for document downloads and exports

### Mobile Access

While Gobify is optimized for desktop use:
- Basic features work on tablets in landscape mode
- Phone access is limited but functional for simple tasks
- For best experience, use a desktop or laptop computer

## Getting Comfortable with Gobify

Now that you understand the basics:

1. **Explore the Dashboard**: Start by reviewing the statistics and KPI cards
2. **Browse Citizens**: Use the search to look up a few citizen records
3. **Check Programs**: View the social programs relevant to your role
4. **Familiarize Yourself**: Click through different sections to see what's available

Remember: You can't break anything by exploring. The system has safeguards in place, and most actions require confirmation before making changes.
